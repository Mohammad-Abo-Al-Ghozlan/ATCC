Road Network Expansion
Completion Date: August 2023
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
Category: Infrastructure
Client: Ministry of Transport

Project Description

Comprehensive road construction and expansion project to alleviate traffic congestion and improve connectivity in the growing urban area. The project included new road construction, intersection improvements, and drainage system upgrades.

Key Features

  • 20 km of new road construction
  • 5 major intersection upgrades
  • Advanced drainage and stormwater management
  • Smart traffic signaling system
